local QuestSystem = {}
QuestSystem.__index = QuestSystem
 
function QuestSystem.new(data)
  local self = setmetatable({}, QuestSystem)
  self._quests = data.quests or {}
  self._active = {}
  return self
end
 
function QuestSystem:assign(id)
  local q = self._quests[id]
  if not q then return false end
  self._active[id] = { progress = 0 }
  return true
end

Terms of Service

By commissioning me, you confirm you have read and accept these terms. They exist to protect both parties.

01

Project Requirements

A clear, written specification is required before work begins. Vague requests will be asked to clarify or offered a paid scoping session. Requirements that change mid-development are treated as separate tasks with a new quote.

02

Payment Policy

50% upfront, 50% on delivery. The agreed price is final — you cover all applicable taxes and transfer fees. Work begins only after the upfront payment has been received and cleared.

Fast-settlement methods (Wise, Remitly) are strongly preferred to avoid ambiguity about when funds have cleared.
03

Refunds

No cancellations once a commission is confirmed and the upfront payment is made. Upfront payments are non-refundable except where I am personally unable to complete the agreed work.

04

Bug Fixes

Bugs in my own code are fixed at no charge. Report issues within 24 hours of delivery — after that window, problems are assumed to result from your own edits. A "bug" is defined as a failure to meet the agreed specification, not a change in requirements.

05

Ownership & Licence

Once 100% of the agreed payment is received, you own the commission outright and may use, resell, or modify it freely. Before full payment, I retain the right to withhold the final handoff. I may display non-confidential work in my portfolio unless we agree otherwise in writing.

06

Communication & 72-Hour Inactivity

Timely replies matter on both sides. If either party does not respond for 72 consecutive hours during negotiation, the commission may be treated as abandoned.

This clause exists to reduce wasted time and protect both parties against bad-faith arrangements.
07

Scope Changes & Disputes

Requests outside the originally agreed scope are new work and quoted separately. Quality disputes must cite specific, reproducible issues in writing within the 24-hour bug fix window.

08

Liability

Work is provided "as is." I am not liable for indirect, consequential, or lost-profit damages, nor for issues caused by your modifications, third-party assets, or changes to the Roblox platform. Total liability is capped at fees paid for that specific commission.
